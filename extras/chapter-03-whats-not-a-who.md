# Chapter 3: What's Not A Who (Who Defines You)

**URL:** https://wishful-tinkering.com.com/pages/whats-not-a-who

## Content Summary:

**Concept:** Name, occupation, beliefs ≠ WHO you are. These are Whats (things you have), not WHOs (your identity).

---

## Key Questions:

**"Who are you?"** → People answer with name, occupation, beliefs... none of these are correct.

**"What's in a name? That which we call a rose, by any other name, would smell as sweet."**
— Shakespeare
