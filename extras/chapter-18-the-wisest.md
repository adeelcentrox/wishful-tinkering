## Key Concepts:

**Wisdom vs Intellect:**
- **Wisdom** = Action of moving mental building blocks, creating new connections
- **Intellect** = The facts, the building blocks themselves
- Wisdom can lead to intellect, but intellect doesn't easily lead to wisdom
- 1 Intellect + 1 Intellect = 2 Intellect (Wisdom is the action of adding)

**The Cement Mix Metaphor:**
- Wisdom is like water - fluid, moving
- Intellect is like cement mix - solid, unchanging
- Too much intellect without wisdom = hardened, immovable mind
- Best ratio: Enough wisdom to keep intellect fluid and adaptable

**Children as Wisest:**
- Born with absolute wisdom but no intellect
- Use small amounts of intelligence to become fully functioning adults
- Childhood wisdom is like a sledgehammer - powerful and direct
