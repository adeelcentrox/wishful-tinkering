## Key Concepts:

**The Chain of Success:**
- Dreams → Planning → Action → Results
- Results are the best units to measure actions by
- Results take place in reality, not in your imagination
- "While actions speak louder than words, results speak louder still"

**Outcasts:**
- Every person of influence is removed from society at large
- **Attractive outcasts**: Others want to do, think, feel what they do (celebrities)
- **Repellent outcasts**: Others want to do the opposite (Hitler)
- Being powerful means becoming an outcast - it's a package deal

**Power as Belt Metaphor:**
- Power holds up your foundations (like a belt holds up pants)
- Belts are everywhere, can be bought anywhere
- We only talk about belts when abused (beating someone with it)
- Same with power - we only notice it when abused
- Knowledge is power - giving advice is handing power to someone
