## Key Concepts:

**Magic as Map:**
- Map of shortcuts and blind spots in human brain
- List of errors in average human brain
- List of weaknesses to modern soul
- Only read by arrogant cons, ruthless frauds... and purely humble magicians

**Thurston's 4 Rules of Magic:**
1. **Never reveal your secrets** - foundations of all lies and magic
2. **Never explain what will happen** - gives destination to look for, they may see the pathway
3. **Practice to perfection** - never perform before ready, or you'll fumble and reveal secrets
4. **Once is a trick, twice is a lesson** - if life does same thing twice, you're supposed to learn from it

**The World as Magician:**
- Rule #1: World never reveals secrets (meaning of life, time left to live)
- Rule #2: World never tells what happens next (future is mystery)
- Rule #3: World never broken, never slipped up (practiced for billions of years)
- Rule #4: Lessons repeat until you learn them

**Key Insights:**
- Magicians don't act in spite of reality, they embrace it MORE deeply
- Magicians are often the most consummate realists
- All magic seems normal until the big reveal
- World helps you out but isn't good at taking credit
